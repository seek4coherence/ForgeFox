# ForgeFox Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 22, 2026**

ForgeFox respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how we handle your data. ForgeFox is a local-first AI coding assistant — your code and data stay under your control.

### **Where Your Data Goes (And Where It Doesn't)**

- **Code & Files**: ForgeFox accesses files on your local machine when needed for AI-assisted features. When you send commands to ForgeFox, relevant files may be transmitted to your chosen AI model provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, OpenRouter) to generate responses. Your code is sent directly to the provider you choose — ForgeFox does not operate any proxy servers or cloud services that handle your code.
- **Commands**: Any commands executed through ForgeFox happen on your local environment. When you use AI-powered features, the relevant code and context may be transmitted to your chosen AI model provider to generate responses. AI providers may process data per their own privacy policies.
- **Prompts & AI Requests**: When you use AI-powered features, your prompts and relevant project context are sent directly to your chosen AI model provider. ForgeFox does not store, process, or intercept this data. These AI providers have their own privacy policies and may store data per their terms of service.
- **API Keys & Credentials**: If you enter an API key (e.g., to connect an AI model), it is stored locally on your device within VS Code's secret storage and is never sent to us or any third party, except the provider you have chosen.
- **Telemetry**: ForgeFox has all telemetry disabled. No usage data, analytics, or error reports are sent to any server. Your usage of ForgeFox is completely private.
- **Local Models**: You can use ForgeFox entirely offline with local models (Ollama, LM Studio, etc.) — in this case, absolutely no data leaves your machine.

### **How We Use Your Data**

- ForgeFox does **not** collect any data from users.
- We do **not** sell or share your data.
- We do **not** train any models on your data.
- We do **not** operate telemetry, analytics, or tracking services.

### **Your Choices & Control**

- You can run models locally to prevent any data from being sent to third parties.
- You choose which AI provider to use and have full control over what data is sent.
- You can uninstall ForgeFox at any time to remove all extension data.

### **Security & Updates**

We take reasonable measures to secure your data, but no system is 100% secure. If our privacy policy changes, we will notify you within the extension.

For any privacy-related questions, reach out to us at support@BusinessEdgeAnalytics.com.
